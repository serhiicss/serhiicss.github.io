Alex Driving School

My first client

Alex Driving School is owned and operated by an old friend of mine and happens to be my first client. My friend opened the school in 2015 and I joined as marketing director a year later.

I have developed a website, setup social media accounts, registered with search and business engines, and started posting engaging content. In the first 6 months the school have doubled the number of clients.

I designed and implemented a client feedback system that allowed clients to leave school feedback on rating websites of their choice. As a result, Alex Driving School has received a large amount of positive feedback from clients and has climbed to number 1 spot in Yelp! search.

  • Start date: March 2017
  • Client: Alex Driving School
  • Category: Personal service
